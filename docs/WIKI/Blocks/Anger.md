# 🧱 Block 1: Anger ✨

**Anger** is the emotional result of demanding that reality, other people, or events obey our internal rules. It is a "resistance emotion," created by arguing with what already exists.

---

## 🧮 **The Anger Formula**

### `A = ET + S`

- **ET (Egocentric Thinking)**: Mistaking our opinions for universal facts.
- **S (Should)**: Attaching a "must" or "should" to an event (e.g., "This should not be happening!").

---

## 🔮 **Core Insight**

Anger is not caused by the person who cut you off in traffic or the partner who forgot an anniversary. It is caused by the demand: **"This MUST not happen."** 

When the demand is removed and replaced with a **Preference** ("I would prefer this didn't happen, but it is"), anger dissolves.

---

## 📜 **Practical Steps to Dissolve Anger**

1.  **Notice**: Feel the physical heat of anger.
2.  **Identify**: Find the "Should" (e.g., "They should be more respectful").
3.  **Challenge**: Ask, "Where is it written that they *must* be respectful?"
4.  **Replace**: Shift to a preference ("I'd like them to be respectful, but they aren't right now").
