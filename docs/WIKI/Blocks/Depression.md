# 🧱 Block 3: Depression ✨

**Depression** is the emotional result of believing that a loss is absolute, that the future is hopeless, and that one's self-worth is tied to what has been lost.

---

## 🧮 **The Depression Formula**

### `D = H1 + H2 + N`

- **H1 (Hopelessness)**: The belief that things will never get better.
- **H2 (Helplessness)**: The belief that there is nothing one can do to change the situation.
- **N (Pseudo-Need)**: Mistaking a *preference* (e.g., for love, success, or health) for a *necessity*.

---

## 🔮 **Core Insight**

Depression often stems from a **Pseudo-Need**. We tell ourselves we *need* a partner's love or a specific career to be okay. When we don't have it, we conclude we are worthless. 

Liberation comes from realizing: **"I want it, but I don't need it to be a happy human being."**

---

## 📜 **Practical Steps to Dissolve Depression**

1.  **Identify the Loss**: What is the "A" (Activating Event)?
2.  **Find the Pseudo-Need**: What do you believe you *must* have to be happy?
3.  **Challenge Worthlessness**: Use the **B=B Equation** (Behavior = Behavior) to separate your "Self" from your "Situation."
4.  **Action**: Engage in **Creative Absorption** (part of the Greater Happiness Formula).
