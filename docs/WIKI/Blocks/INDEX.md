# 🧱 The Four Blocks ✨

In the **My 4 Blocks** methodology, all human emotional suffering can be distilled into four specific thinking habits or "blocks." These are more than just feelings; they are the primary obstacles to joy and contentment.

---

## 🎪 **The Cast of Disturbances**

| Block | The Core Demand | The Formula |
|-------|-----------------|-------------|
| [**Anger**](../Blocks/Anger.md) | "Reality *must* obey my rules!" | `A = ET + S` |
| [**Anxiety**](../Blocks/Anxiety.md) | "The future *must* be certain and safe!" | `Anx = WI + AW + ICSI` |
| [**Depression**](../Blocks/Depression.md) | "I *must* have what I cannot have!" | `D = H1 + H2 + N` |
| [**Guilt**](../Blocks/Guilt.md) | "I *must* be perfect, or I am worthless!" | `G = W1 + W2` |

---

## 🔮 **Core Principles**

- **Internal Causation**: Emotions are not caused by external events, but by our *beliefs* about those events.
- **The Racket**: Every seeker has a "personal racket"—their favorite bad feeling that they choose most often when upset.
- **Resistance to Reality**: All four blocks are forms of arguing with "what is."
- **The Solution**: Transitioning from **Demands** to **Preferences**.
