# 🧮 The ABC Model ✨

The **ABC Model** is the foundational framework for understanding how emotions are created. Developed by **Dr. Albert Ellis**, it is the core engine of **Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy (REBT)**.

---

## 🌟 **The Variables of the Mind**

| Symbol | Name | Description |
|--------|------|-------------|
| **A** | **Activating Event** | The trigger—a situation, a person's behavior, or even a thought. |
| **B** | **Belief** | The internal monologue—the 60,000+ sentences we say to ourselves. |
| **C** | **Consequences** | The result—emotional, behavioral, and physiological responses. |

---

## 🔮 **The Evolution to ABCDE**

To move beyond understanding and into liberation, we add two critical steps:

- **D**: **Disputing** — The act of red-flagging and challenging irrational beliefs.
- **E**: **Effective Belief** — The replacement of demands with rational preferences.

---

## 📜 **The Key Insight**

**A does not cause C.** 
Most people live in a state of "A-to-C Thinking," blaming circumstances for their misery. The truth is that **B (your belief)** is the filter that transforms an event into an emotion.
