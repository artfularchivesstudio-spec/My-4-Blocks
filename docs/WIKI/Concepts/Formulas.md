# 🧮 The Formulas of Disturbance ✨

In the **My 4 Blocks** framework, each primary emotional disturbance is represented by a formula. These formulas help seekers identify the specific irrational components of their thinking.

---

## 🧱 **The Block Formulas**

### 1. Anger
`A = ET + S`
- **ET**: Egocentric Thinking (Opinions = Facts)
- **S**: Should/Must (Demand for compliance)

### 2. Anxiety
`Anx = WI + AW + ICSI`
- **WI**: What If (Future projection)
- **AW**: Awfulizing (Catastrophizing)
- **ICSI**: I Can't Stand It (Low frustration tolerance)

### 3. Depression
`D = H1 + H2 + N`
- **H1**: Hopelessness (Fixed future)
- **H2**: Helplessness (Lack of agency)
- **N**: Pseudo-Need (Desire treated as necessity)

### 4. Guilt
`G = W1 + W2`
- **W1**: Wrongness (Global rating of behavior)
- **W2**: Worthlessness (Global rating of self)

---

## 🌟 **The Happiness Formulas**

### Long Happiness Formula
`H = USA + UAOW + SN`
- **USA**: [[Unconditional Self-Acceptance]]
- **UAOW**: Unconditional Acceptance of Others and the World
- **SN**: Staying Now (Present moment awareness)

### Greater Happiness Formula
`GH = H + CA - HF`
- **H**: Basic Happiness
- **CA**: Creative Absorption (Flow)
- **HF**: Hassle Factor (Trivial disturbances)
