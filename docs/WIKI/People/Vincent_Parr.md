# 🧙‍♂️ Dr. Vincent E. Parr, Ph.D. ✨

**Dr. Vincent Parr** is the founder of the **Parr Institute for Mental Wellness** and the author of the seminal work, *"You Only Have Four Problems: The Cure for Anger, Anxiety, Depression & Guilt"* (2021).

With over 50 years of clinical experience, Dr. Parr distilled the thousands of problems his patients presented into just four fundamental "blocks" to happiness.

---

## 🌟 **Lineage & Mastery**

Dr. Parr's work is a masterful synthesis of two seemingly disparate worlds:

1.  **Rational Emotive Behavior Therapy (REBT)**: Mentored by the creator of REBT, [**Dr. Albert Ellis**](../People/Albert_Ellis.md).
2.  **Zen Buddhism**: Mentored by Zen Priest [**Steve Hagen**](../People/Steve_Hagen.md).

---

## 🔮 **Key Contributions**

- **The Four Blocks**: Identified [Anger](../Blocks/Anger.md), [Anxiety](../Blocks/Anxiety.md), [Depression](../Blocks/Depression.md), and [Guilt](../Blocks/Guilt.md) as the primary disturbances.
- **The Racket**: Introduced the concept of the "personal racket"—our favorite bad feeling that we choose most often.
- **The Formulas**: Created simple algebraic representations for each emotional disturbance to make them observable and disputable.
- **The Sanctuary**: Envisioned a digital home for these teachings, which has evolved into the **My 4 Blocks** project.
